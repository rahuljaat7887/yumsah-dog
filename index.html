Hi, my name is Humberto, I am the current owner and head trainer for Outrageous Dog Training LLC. I currently share my home with a 12 year old rescued pitbull named Angelica Pickles and a 2 year old rat terrier/ poodle mix named Stella. I have worked in the field for well over 12 years using positive reinforcement methods and balance training methods and pride myself on knowing if and when to use appropriate training tools. I enjoy trick training but my specialty is helping those with fear, anxiety, aggression, reactivity, behavioral modification and rehabilitation. I am passionate about strengthening the human-animal bond through training, playing, and a structured lifestyle.

Private Sessions

Our one hour private sessions are ideal for those who would like to be more involved in the training process. Our goal is to educate you on how dogs think and why they do what they do. We teach you to train your companion basic commands and problem solve any unwanted behaviors. Private sessions are tailored to the individual client and dogs needs, from leash pulling and potty training to aggression and leash reactivity. Sessions are typically done once a week and owners will be given homework assignments after each session, which should be done a minimum of 45 minutes a day throughout the day.

Board & Train

This program offers one on one attention, socialization, day trips, all while setting good habits and obedience. Your companion will stay with an experienced trainer for 1 to 4 weeks and learn basic commands; as well as, basic house manners. Our goal is to help solve any unwanted behaviors such as jumping on guests, annoying barking, chewing, door dashing, leash reactivity, behavior modification, fears and anxieties. We will work to transfer all training to the owners and educate them on how to properly reinforce wanted behaviors and how to extinguish unwanted nuisance behaviors.

Day Training and Socialization

This program is for those who have already completed a training package. A trainer will pick up and train, socialize your companion in the real world. We can do the tedious repetitions of known commands at a local park, hiking trail, or a pet friendly store. This is also an ideal package to properly socialize your young or fearful companion.
